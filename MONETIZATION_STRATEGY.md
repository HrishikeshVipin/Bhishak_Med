**Model:** Commission on lab test bookings

**How it Works:**
1. Add "Book Lab Test" feature in platform

## üö´ What We Will NOT Do

### No Display Advertising
**Why:**
- ‚ùå Destroys professional brand image
- ‚ùå Minimal revenue (‚Çπ2,000-5,000/month for our traffic)
- ‚ùå Can't control ad content (dating apps, gambling, etc.)
- ‚ùå Paying doctors shouldn't see ads
- ‚ùå Patients seeking medical help deserve ad-free experience

**Industry Standard:** Practo, 1mg, PharmEasy, Apollo don't use display ads

---

### No Commission on Doctor Consultations (For Paid Tiers)
**Why:**
- ‚úÖ Doctors already paying subscription
- ‚úÖ Keep 100% of their consultation fees
- ‚úÖ Aligns our incentives with theirs (we grow when they grow)
- ‚úÖ Competitive advantage vs commission-based platforms

**Exception:** Free tier doctors (future) may have commission model

---

### No Patient Signup Fees
**Why:**
- ‚úÖ Acquisition barrier for patients
- ‚úÖ Doctors bring patients, not vice versa
- ‚úÖ Network effects (more patients = more valuable for doctors)

**Monetize patients via:** Premium features, not signup fees
